## Business Classification

**What We Are:** Tech-Enabled Accounting Services / Accounting Automation Agency

| Category | Status | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| **Professional Services** | ✅ Yes | We deliver bookkeeping and accounting services for clients |
| **B2B Services** | ✅ Yes | Monthly retainer, client relationships, managed service delivery |
| **Accounting Automation Consultancy** | ✅ Yes | We build custom automation tailored to each client's workflow |
| **SaaS** | ❌ No | We don't sell software subscriptions; clients don't use a product themselves |
| **Fintech** | ❌ No | We don't process payments, provide loans, or build consumer financial apps |
| **Software Company** | ❌ No | Our tools support service delivery, they're not products we sell |

**Simple Definition:**
> TechHarbor is an accounting automation agency. We handle bookkeeping for small businesses and build custom automation systems that give them real-time financial visibility — without hiring extra staff.

**For LLC Registration:** Professional Services / Consulting

**For Insurance:** Professional Liability (E&O)

**Future Optionality:** If we extract automation into a self-service platform ("TechHarbor Lite"), we'd become a hybrid services + SaaS company. Currently pure services.

## Compliance & Legal Wording

### ✅ APPROVED Terminology

Use these terms freely (legal for bookkeeping/automation services):

- Financial systems
- Accounting operations
- Accounting automation
- Bookkeeping services
- Financial reporting
- Reconciliation services
- Automated workflows
- Finance engineering
- Custom accounting solutions
- Real-time financial insights

### ⚠️ AVOID These Terms

Do NOT use (requires licenses/registrations):

- ❌ Financial advisory services
- ❌ Wealth management
- ❌ Investment advice
- ❌ Tax preparation (unless partnering with licensed CPA)
- ❌ Audit services
- ❌ CPA services (Jay has Philippine CPA, not US CPA)

### Compliance-Safe Service Description

> "TechHarbor provides bookkeeping, accounting automation, and managed financial systems. We engineer and maintain workflows for transaction recording, reconciliations, payroll processing, and real-time financial reporting. We do not provide audit, tax preparation, or investment advisory services."

## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Target Audience

- Small business owners (5-50 employees)
- Construction, staffing, service businesses
- $500K-$5M annual revenue
- Forward-thinking but practical

### Tone

- Professional but approachable
- Technical expertise without jargon
- Results-focused (time saved, accuracy, visibility)
- "We build it FOR you" (not DIY software)

### Key Differentiators

1. **CPA + Developer Hybrid** - Accounting knowledge + technical skills in one service
1. **Custom Built** - Not generic software, tailored to YOUR business
1. **Real-Time Visibility** - Dashboards, not month-old reports
1. **No Extra Headcount** - Get accuracy without hiring full accounting team

### Value Propositions

**Primary:**
- "Gear Up. Scale Higher."
- "In the age of AI, don't get left behind."

**Supporting:**
- "Accurate books. Real-time visibility. No extra headcount."
- "Where accounting meets automation."
- "Engineered financial systems for growing businesses."
- "Your CPA who codes."

**Positioning Statement:**
> In this age of AI and automation, businesses that don't adapt will be left behind. TechHarbor helps forward-thinking business owners harness the power of technology — automating the tedious, surfacing real-time insights, and building systems that scale. We're not just accountants. We're your technology partners for smarter, faster growth.
