## Filing Steps (INBiz)

1. Go to Indiana **INBiz** → start a new business filing.
2. Choose **Domestic Limited Liability Company (LLC)**.
3. Enter **Entity Name** exactly: **Tech Harbor Financial Systems, LLC**.
4. Registered Agent:
   - Type: **Individual**
   - Name + Indiana street address (Ben)
5. Principal Office Address:
   - Use Ben’s Indiana home street address.
6. Management:
   - **Member-managed**
7. Organizer:
   - Jay (the person submitting the form)
8. Submit + pay **$95**.

## 5) Immediately After Approval

### EIN (IRS) — free

- Apply for EIN with the exact same legal name.

### Operating Agreement (not filed with the state)

- Write/sign an operating agreement.

### Banking

- Open business bank account using:
  - Indiana formation approval
  - EIN confirmation

### Wise Business (optional — spend controls/subscriptions)

- Open a **Wise Business** account under the LLC (keep Wise Personal separate).
- Use it for subscriptions/online purchases if needed.

## 6) Cost Summary (typical)

- Indiana LLC online filing: **$95**
- EIN: **$0**
- Registered agent (Ben): **$0/year**
- Ongoing state compliance (biennial report): typically **~$32 online / ~$50 paper** (budget as recurring)
